What is NT porchfest ?

We are a community driven project ! Every year we put together an event to celebrate Music, Performance and whatever else seems to fit. Unlike most events that are hosted at one specific venue NT porch fest is held on the lawns and porches of our community giving people a chance to come together and explore while discovering all the amazing talent peppered across our neighborhood.
