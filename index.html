What is NT porchfest ?

We are a community driven project ! Every year we put together an event to celebrate Music, Performance and whatever else seems to fit. Unlike most events that are hosted at one specific venue NT porch fest is held on the lawns and porches of our community giving people a chance to come together and explore while discovering all the amazing talent peppered across our neighborhood.

The Matts Music Charitable Foundation
Check out our friends at Matts Music!

Since its inception, the mission of Matt's Music has always been to help others find their connection to the eternal gifts that music provides. Our unwavering commitment to this goal means that the clients we serve take priority above everything else. To this end, the Matt's Music Charitable Foundation is the latest extension of this mission. The Matt's Music Charitable Foundation is a non-profit, 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to creating accessibility to music and music education for all, regardless of physical, economic, or social obstacles.
